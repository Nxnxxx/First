Ingrediants for sesame chicken

  • 10 ozs skinless and boneless chicken breast
  • 2 tsps rice wine
  • 2 egg whites, lightly beaten
  • salt
  • 2 tbs cornstartch mixed with 2 tbs water
  • 1/4 lb ground walnuts (see box, right)
  • 1/4 cup sesame seeds
  • Ciruander (cilantro) sprigs and tomato roses (below) for garnish

How to make the sesame chicken

  1. Cut the chicken across the grain into thin slices. Put the slices in a dish, add the rice wine and 1/2 tsp salt, and stir well to mix. Cover and let stand at room temperature for 25-30 minutes.
  2. Blend the egg whites with the cornstartch paste. Mix together the walnuts and sesame seeds. Coat the chicken first with the cornstarch, then with the nuts and seeds. Chill uncovered, in the refridgerator for about 1 hour.
  3. Heat the oil in a wok until moderately hot (325ËšF), and deep-fry the chickenin batches for about 2 minutes until crisp, and golden. Remove and drain. Serve hot, garnished with coriander sprigs and tomato roses.
